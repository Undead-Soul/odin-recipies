Knorr Soup - Recipe

Description

We can cook it in nearly 15-20 minutes. There is no need for you to have cooking skills. Just follow the instructions below you can make the awesome tasty soup all by yourself.

Ingredients

  • 400ml water
  • coriander
  • teaspoon of ghee
  • Knorr Tomato Soup

Steps

  1. Place the vessel on the stove
  2. Add the contents of the Pack to the cooking vessel
  3. Add some water to the cooking vessel and mix it well so that no lumps are formed, then add some water, mix it well. repeat till the water is poured completely to the vessel
  4. Add the tea spoon of ghee to the knorr mixed water
  5. Now light up the stove, keep the flame at max
  6. Stir with spoon, Do not stop stirring, becoz if u do so, it may flow out if u are using a small vessel
  7. stop when you feel that it is thickened enough
  8. Add coriander if u want now it is time to turn off the stove
  9. Now por the soup to the bowl to eat
  10. Make sure to eat while its still hot
