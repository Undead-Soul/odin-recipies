Maggi - Recipe

Description

We can cook it in nearly 15-30 minutes. There is no need for you to have cooking skills. Just follow the instructions below you can make the awesome tasty noodles all by yourself.

Ingredients

  • 250ml water
  • coriander
  • teaspoon of ghee
  • Maggi Noodles worth Rs 40
  • seasonings like oreango and chilli flakes

Steps

  1. Place the vesel on the stove
  2. Add water to the cooking vessel
  3. Add Masala given in the Maggi Packet to the water
  4. Add the tea spoon of ghee to the masala mixed water
  5. Break the noodle cake cake into 2 parts so that it can soak completely in water
  6. Now light up the stove, keep the flame at max
  7. When you can see that most of the water was evaporated, you can add seasonings
  8. Stir it so that it mixes well
  9. now it is time to turn off the stove (No water can be seen)
  10. Now transfer these noodles to the bowl
  11. Make sure to eat while its still hot
